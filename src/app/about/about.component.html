About Me

Jordan Danberry

The Influencer

“If I could choose who I could inspire, I’d choose the next generations of entrepreneurs, leaders and innovators. I know I can’t ‘just pick’ the people I inspire, but I want my influence to, you know, reach those crowds.”

The President

Jordan’s leadership on and off the court led her to becoming the President of Conducting Creativity, a 501c3 non-profit centered around literacy, innovation and entrepreneurship. As President, she has directed and oversaw programs such as Skillz and Drillz Basketball and Entreprenuership Camp, literacy tours, and feeding programs.
The Skillz School is a mentee/mentor based program where new and experienced learners have the opportunity to share their knowledge base and expertise in aims to build the next wave of innovators. As President of Conducting Creativity, Jordan’s role is that of a point guard--making sure operations flow and the team is on the same page.

The Athlete

Jordan is a nationally renowned athlete. The 2015 graduate and native of Conway High School in Central Arkansas then went on to play for the University of Arkansas, Fayetteville, before transferring to Mississippi State University. Below are Jordan’s ancelades since high school.

  • Gatorade Player of the year 2x 2014/2015
  • 2017 and 2018 Division 1 National Championship Finalist
  • ALL SEC Second Team
  • ALL SEC Defensive Team
  • Naismith Player of the Year Midseason team
  • Naismith Defensive POY Semifinalist
  • Elite 90 Award
